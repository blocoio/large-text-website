Write when you can't be heard

Large text turns your phone into a signboard. LT is an Android app that lets you communicate in writing when your voice can't be heard. Your phone or tablet becomes a sign with the message you want.

How it works

① Write a message
② Pick a theme (simple or a colorful)
③ Press play
④ Your message will be displayed fullscreen

What can you do?

Supports text and emojis

Multiple themes (some are paid)

Save to gallery and Share them in others apps

App shortcuts for your last messages

Portrait and landscape mode

History of the messages
